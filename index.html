About

My Upbringing.

  • Oct 2013 - Sept 2014

    My Humble Beginning

    After transfering from a community college in New York, I decided to take myself away from my hometown and pursue a web degree at Full Sail University. I always had a knack for technology and wanted to be apart of this booming field of study. I knew it would be a challenge but I love pushing myself to the limits and exceeding my own expectations. I was very eager to start this new chapter in my life.

  • Oct 2014 - May 2015

    A Passion Develops

    As I got through my introduction level classes I really started to dig deep into my study. I wanted to keep learning outside of the classroom. At first, I started building static web sites for my friends and family and utilizing every I have learned at this point. I felt as if I kept practicing I would eventually become a pro, and wanted to become the best I could possibly be in my field.

  • June 2015 - Present

    Shaping Into a Developer

    After getting to know my programming strategy and knowing my abilities, I decided that this is what I want to do for a living. I now can say that I am confident in being a part of a company and being a valuable asset. I love working as a team, and like to think of myself as a leader. With my experience at this point I know the ups and downs of a web developer and built a strategy on top of these experiences. I look further into the future and can see a bright path.
